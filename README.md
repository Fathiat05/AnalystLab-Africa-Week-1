Key Insights
1. United Kingdom Dominates Sales Performance
The United Kingdom generated approximately 8.2 million in sales, contributing the largest share of total revenue. This indicates that the business relies heavily on the UK market, while other countries contribute significantly less revenue.
2. Dotcom Postage Is the Best-Selling Product
Dotcom Postage was the top-selling product with approximately 206K in sales, followed by Regency Cakestand 3 Tier and White Hanging Heart T-Light Holder. These products are key revenue drivers and play an important role in overall business performance.
3. Strong Seasonal Sales Trend
Sales increased toward the end of the year, with the highest revenue recorded in November and December. This suggests a strong seasonal effect, likely driven by holiday shopping and year-end promotions.
